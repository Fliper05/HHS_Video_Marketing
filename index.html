Recording

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to the most frequently asked questions. If you still have questions, please contact us using the information below this section.

Is this video really free?

Yes. It is completely free for you to use wherever you desire. Our goal is to empower students with real-world experience working for a company like yours. In return, you get a free marketing video to use however you like. However, if you'd like to make a donation to Video Production program, we would be grateful. It is a win-win.

I really love the video that you created for my business, is there any way that I can show my appreciation?

We are really glad that you like your video. Since our Video Production program heavily relies on grants from the district to cover things such as tripods, light bulbs for lights, cameras, lenses, software, microphones, green screens, and so on. A donation to our program would help a ton, so that we get to empower our students with the right tools for them to fully express their own potential.

What rights do I get with the video?

After your video is finished and delivered to you, you will receive a full commercial license. Therefore you can use it anywhere on social media to represent your business, and even on the TV or other public media.

I have contacted HHS Video Marketing Services to get a video, how long does it take for the video to be delivered?

We want to make sure that everything is to your liking. From the time we start working on your video, it should take approximately 2 weeks for it to be delivered.
